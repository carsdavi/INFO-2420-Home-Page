    Answer:

    How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)


    Answer:

    They have bar that is on the left corner you can click on to open a tabloid of information on their products, recipes, etc. The top of the website has location status, information about the store, career opportunities, and announcements.

    The main page includes their newly advertised products they want to showcase and push, that even includes recipes that include the newly added items to help market it.


    Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?


    Answer:

    The site structure makes sense because of how they structured their website to showcase all of their new items right on the new page, as stated earlier, they had a page link to direct customers to recipes with all of the featured items.

    That creates more interaction within the site and is a great way to get customers in the stores. The structure is chock-full of bits and links that are placed in the way to create a "flow of traffic" with the customer.
